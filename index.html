Who We Are

Sun & Spear Studios

We build software in pursuit of a better world — not because it is simple, but because it matters.

Sun & Spear Studios is an independent software studio founded on a straightforward idea: the skills that build great products can be pointed at something good. We are not defined by any single category of software — our work spans civic education, personal privacy, and everyday utility. What unites every project is the belief that technology, done simply and honestly, can leave the world a little better than we found it.

We build with intention. Our projects don't chase trends or scale for its own sake. Each one begins with a question: does this genuinely help someone understand something, protect something, or simplify something? If the answer is yes, we get to work.

Sun & Spear is founded on simplicity and the principle of giving back — structurally, not as an afterthought. From day one, every dollar our products earn flows through a framework designed to sustain our work, reward our team, and invest meaningfully in the causes we care about most.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I win my first election? ▾
Focus on building support with the voter factions most prevalent in your district. Manage your campaign finances carefully, invest in name recognition early, and don't neglect your ground game. Starting on Easy difficulty is recommended for first-time players.
What is a "voter faction" and why does it matter? ▾
Voter factions are the 22 distinct ideological and demographic groups that make up the electorate. Each faction has unique policy priorities and responds differently to your actions. Understanding and balancing faction relationships is the core strategic challenge of the game.
Can I run as an independent or third-party candidate? ▾
Yes. While running as a major party candidate provides structural advantages (party infrastructure, easier ballot access), independent and third-party runs are possible and represent a unique strategic challenge with different coalition-building requirements.
How does opposition research work? ▾
Your Opposition Research staff will uncover vulnerabilities in your opponent's record over time. You can then choose to use this information in debate attacks, media campaigns, or hold it in reserve. Be aware — your opponents are conducting research on you too.
How do I save my game progress? ▾
Of The People features automatic save functionality. Your progress is saved at key milestones including after elections, legislative sessions, and major campaign events. You can also manually save from the main menu at any time.
Is this game suitable for younger players? ▾
Of The People is designed as an educational civic simulation appropriate for players 12 and older. The game contains no graphic content, and its focus on democratic processes makes it a valuable learning tool for students and civics enthusiasts of all ages.
I found a bug. How do I report it? ▾
Please use our Contact page to submit a bug report. Include a description of what happened, what you expected to happen, and your device model and operating system version. We review all reports and release regular updates.
